photography

  • Photography is an art form that involves capturing light with a camera to create a visual representation of a moment or scene. This art form has evolved significantly since its inception in the early 19th century and continues to evolve as technology advances.

    photography is art

  • Photography not only preserves memories but also tells stories and conveys messages. A skilled photographer can use composition, lighting, and other techniques to create an image that captures the essence of a subject and evokes emotion in the viewer.
